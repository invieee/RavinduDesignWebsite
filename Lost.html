LOST

Zine
Ristoprint exploration
2023

As I grow as an artist, the influences I take from and mediums I use, evolve and change with me. The personal brand called ‘lost’ has come to represent my personality, from its early stages in 2018, trying to be cool and edgy with its ironic use of the strike-through and almost non-existent meaning.

I cringe, yet reminisce about those simpler times. This zine aims to reinforce my identity as a designer through the brand LOST, using an updated skill set and four years of development.
